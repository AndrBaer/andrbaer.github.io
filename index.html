Biography

Hello there, and welcome to my personal website! 🔥

I am a final-year PhD student in Electrical Engineering / Computer Science under supervision of Tim Fingscheidt, Professor at the Institute for Communications Technology, Technische Universität Braunschweig. My research interests are in the area of computer vision with focus on:

  • Semantic Segmentation
  • Out-of-Distribution Robustness, Adversarial Attacks
  • Teacher-Student Learning
  • Performance Prediction
Education

  • MSc in Electrical Engineering, 2018

Projects

Single-Task Multi-Decoder Network Ensembles
Investigation of methods for creating realistically looking universal adversarial attacks for semantic segmentation on video data.
KI Absicherung - Safe AI for Automated Driving
Large national project with a consortium of both industry and academia. The focus lied on establishing a stringend and provable safety argumentation for AI-based functions.
Homepage
Universal Adversarial Attacks on Videos
Investigation and implementation of ensemble-based semantic segmentation methods while looking at performance, floating point operations, execution time, and out-of-distribution robustness.
Adversarial Robustness of Teacher-Student Networks
Investigation of methods for teacher-student networks performing semantic segmentation under the context of adversarial robustness.
